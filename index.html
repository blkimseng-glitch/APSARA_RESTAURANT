Our Story

Living Cambodian Cuisine

APSARA is a high-end Cambodian restaurant that prides itself on offering Living Cambodian Cuisine. Through dedicated research, practice, and promotion of Cambodian culinary traditions, APSARA strives to preserve the nation's rich food heritage for generations to come.

Our cuisine draws inspiration from the ancient Angkor period, offering a unique flavour profile created through a masterful blend of rare spices and herbs. Each dish reflects Cambodia's rich botanical heritage — a story told through taste.

Our Venues

APSARA Phnom Penh

APSARA serves Living Cambodian Cuisine in a tranquil courtyard with flowing fish pond, greeneries & intricate architecture surrounded by indoor seating. Open for breakfast, lunch and dinner, APSARA also offers private dining rooms for business lunches or special events.

APSARA Siem Reap

Located in the heart of Siem Reap, just minutes from the iconic Angkor Wat, APSARA Siem Reap is a culinary gem that celebrates Living Cambodian Cuisine. Its peaceful terrace, surrounded by lush greenery, offers a tranquil setting to enjoy the rich flavors of traditional Cambodian dishes.
